# Form Widget Examples

Embeddable form widgets that add a floating button to any website. Click the button to open your FormBlade form.

## Styles

- **[popup.html](popup.html)** — centered modal overlay
- **[slide-in.html](slide-in.html)** — panel slides in from the right
- **[corner-tab.html](corner-tab.html)** — vertical tab on the right edge, opens a card

## Usage

1. Copy the `<script>` tag from any example
2. Paste it before `</body>` on your website
3. Replace the `FORM_URL` with your FormBlade endpoint

## Customisation

- `BUTTON_TEXT` — label on the floating button
- `BUTTON_COLOR` — any CSS colour value
- Position — change `bottom:24px;right:24px` to move the button

The widget loads your form design (fields, theme, colours) from FormBlade. No styling needed on your end.
